Selected Projects

Research tools and open-source implementations for program analysis and machine learning.

  • VarDeterminismChecker — detecting sources of nondeterminism in unsupervised learning implementations
  • Ocelot — verifying nondeterminism/inconsistency via SMT solving
  • Constraint-guided Generalization — MILP-based post-hoc constraints for prediction flips/confidence
  • DeAnomalyzer — reducing nondeterminism/inconsistency in anomaly detection
  • ACE — parallelizing clustering implementations
